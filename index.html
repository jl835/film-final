Cool fight scenes galore, action movies that I watched through Netflix. WARNING! Spoilers ahead!

The Gray Man

Fountain

Starring Ryan Gosling as an agent, The Gray Man has Six(the main character) try to expose corruption as well as protect the ones he care about from merceneries. The details are a bit fuzzy to me, due to a not too memorable plot in my opinion. The last fight scene between the protagonist and antagonist in the fountain was enjoyable at least.

The Fable: The Killer Who Doesn't Kill

Construction Destruction

A sequel to the The Fable, the main character Akira Soto starring Jun'ichi Okada, is a John Wick style of competent when it comes to skill. Although slow at some parts, the fight scene in the construction scaffolding made it all worth it at the near end. With his deadpan face, Okada made the scene great (and he does his own stunts).

Extraction 2

Firefighting

The sequel to Extration (which I haven't watched), starring Chris Hemsworth, Extraction 2 is a fun watch even without context from the first. Although action pact throughout the whole film, the fight scene in the prison yard was an experience, especially the beatdown after his fists were alight.
